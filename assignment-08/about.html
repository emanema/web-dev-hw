Why the croptop?

I tried to think of something easy that I do a lot and at first settled on tying shoes but I decided that was too common. Then I remembered last semester when all my friends and I would buy cheap Griz t-shirts from Walmart and crop them into something cuter, thus, how to crop a t-shirt. For me, cropping a t-shirt is a right of passage, an initiation if you will, into the sisterhood of being a young college woman. You can learn more on Pinterest AND in the following video!
